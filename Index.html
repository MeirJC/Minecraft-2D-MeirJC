Welcome to 2D Minecraft

you have an open world, feel free to take out elemnts and place them whereever you like. notice though that you can only place elements in cells not contained by other elements. and that there are 4 DIFFERENT worlds to explore (picked randomly) and over 16,000,000 (!) background options!

you have 3 tools in this game:

  • Axe - Used to mine Wood elements
  • PikeAxe - Used to mine Stone elements
  • shovel - Used to mine Soil elements

once you mined an element it gets into the inventory and you maust use it before minind a new element
